DownHill Works (DH Works), is a mountain bike component manufacturer based out of San Luis Obispo, CA. The company was founded in 2004 and has grown to support a large portion of the aftermarket mountain bike part market, especially on the west coast. DH Works uses CNC milling to manufacture most of their billet aluminum parts making them a premium outfitter with a reputation for quality performance parts. They are a high quality and reliable manufacturer with products aimed towards mountain bikers looking for longer lasting and better performing components.
